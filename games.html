Platform: Nintendo Famicom

Language: Japanese

Comment: This game adds the action game element into a RPG game when Japanese RPG games were turn base only at that time. Players have to solve quests and kill monsters in real time which requires certain controlling skill to complete. Not to mention that some puzzle must be solved using special item in specific place. I admit that this game is a legend.

Baldur's Gate

Platform: PC

Language: English

This game is an excellent D&D action RPG game. Player must form a party to solve the quest. Since it follows the D&D rule, player can be a good or evil character and your teammates also classified with the fame system. It's interesting when a good and evil characters in a same party, one of them may leave randomly. And your actions may affect your fame. NPCs may have different response based on your fame. Not to mention it's really easy to die in the game. So.... save your progress before move to a new map, even it's a town.

Rating:
82%
